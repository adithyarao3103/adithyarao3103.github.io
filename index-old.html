I am Adithya, and I am a physicist.
I am a masters' student at NIT Surat, and currently I am working with Dr Laurent Baulieu, Sorbonne University on my masters' thesis.

My favourite pastime involves indulging in the exquisite art of contemplation, as I delve into the innermost workings of the universe at its tiniest scales, surrendering to the cosmic ballet that dances upon the subatomic stage. would be a fancy way of telling that my field of interest is Quantum Field Theories.
I am currently working on Quantum Field Theories of Strongly Interacting Fields. These theories with their rich mathematics, and beautiful geometries are hugely unexplored owing to their complexity, and here I am, already neck deep in these uncharted waters.

My awe for the universe doesn't make me less sensitive to the man-made wonders, or what we call computers. I belong to that (not so) creamy layer of people who love both physics and programming. Depending on my mood, I am either a theoretical physicist, or a computational physicist working on Lattice Simulations of these field theories. Previously I have also worked with Dr Georg Bergner as a DAAD WISE exchange student at Jena University on Lattice Simulations of BFSS model.
