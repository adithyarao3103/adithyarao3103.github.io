I am Adithya, and I am a physicist.
I am a masters' degree holder from NIT Surat with a specialisation in Physics.
My favourite activity involves indulging in the exquisite art of contemplation, as I dive into the innermost workings of the universe at its tiniest scales, surrendering to the cosmic ballet that dances upon the subatomic stage. would be a fancy way of telling that my field of interest is Quantum Field Theories and Quantum Gravity.
